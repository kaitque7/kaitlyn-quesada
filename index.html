Becoming Myself

This is a year of intentional reinvention — shaping a life that reflects who I truly am. Not trends. Not what others want to see. It's about honoring my instincts, my aesthetics, and the version of myself I've been quietly growing into.

Every choice, every detail, every hour is part of a life I'm building with care and without apology. This is a chapter where I create a world that feels like home — on my terms and in my voice.

— Kaitlyn

About Kaitlyn

Built in operations
rooted in people

Born in Phoenix, Arizona and raised in Tampa since 2006, Kaitlyn Quesada built her career through discipline, structure, and a real investment in the people around her. Her leadership style is practical and high standard, focused on what holds up when the pressure is real.

Since entering real estate in 2019, she has specialized in the part of the business most people ignore: the infrastructure. At Avenue Homes, she partners directly with Mariam Serra, Founder and CEO, to translate vision into execution. Culture stays protected, operations get stronger, and growth stays intentional.

As Chief Operating Officer, Kaitlyn oversees internal systems, agent experience, training frameworks, and operational performance across offices. She is focused on building organizations that feel elevated, stable, and human.
