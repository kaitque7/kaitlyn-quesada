About Kaitlyn

From the ground up —
built to lead

Born in Phoenix, Arizona and raised in Tampa since 2006, Kaitlyn Quesada is a first-generation leader who has built her career through relentless commitment, operational clarity, and a genuine investment in the people around her. She is not simply a product of the real estate industry — she is actively reshaping it from the inside out.

When Kaitlyn entered real estate in 2019, she saw something beyond the transaction: an industry hungry for stronger systems, more intentional leadership, and agents who were truly supported. That vision became her life's work. Her ascent at Avenue Homes is a testament to what happens when purpose meets execution.

As Chief Operating Officer of Avenue Homes, Kaitlyn now oversees the organizational infrastructure, strategic initiatives, and the cultural fabric that holds a high-performing team together — the force behind the structure that allows agents to flourish.

2019 Licensed Realtor Earned her real estate license and began building her foundation in the industry.
